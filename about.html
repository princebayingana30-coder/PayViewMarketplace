About PayView Marketplace

Connecting property owners with buyers and renters across Africa

Our Mission

PayView Marketplace was founded with a vision to revolutionize property transactions in Africa, starting with Rwanda. We provide a secure, transparent platform where property owners can showcase their listings and buyers can discover their perfect property with confidence.

Our mission is to make property transactions accessible, secure, and efficient for everyone, while supporting local economies and fostering trust in the marketplace.

Why We Started

In many African markets, property transactions can be challenging due to lack of transparency, limited access to information, and security concerns. PayView Marketplace addresses these issues by providing:

  • Verified listings from trusted owners
  • Secure payment options including Mobile Money
  • Direct communication channels
  • Transparent pricing and availability
  • Access-controlled viewing for privacy and security
